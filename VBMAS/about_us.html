About Us

We are a team of 3rd year Computer Science Engineering (CSE) students working on a mini project on Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management. Our project aims to develop a system that assists users in managing vehicle breakdown situations efficiently.

The system will provide features such as requesting assistance, locating nearby service providers, tracking the status of assistance requests, and facilitating communication between users and service providers.

To ensure the success of our project, we will utilize our knowledge of programming languages, web development, and database management. We are excited to apply our skills and work collaboratively to deliver a robust and user-friendly solution.
